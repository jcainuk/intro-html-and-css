Apollo the Cat

Hello, my name is Apollo

Hello my name is Apollo and I am the floofiest floof that ever floofed!

I was born in Flooftown in the country of Floofland on the continent of Floofia.

My hobbies are chasing after birds, chasing after insects, getting into fights with snails, inviting other cats to come to my house and pretending I am a stray so that everyone on the street feeds me.

I am super-strong and like jumping through people's upstairs open windows.

My magic powers include hypnosis, mind-control and teleportation.

I liked lying on expensive laptops. The more expensive the better.
